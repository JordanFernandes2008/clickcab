Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a cab?
You can book a cab through our website, mobile app, or by calling us at +91 97022 90804. Booking takes less than 2 minutes.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and all major digital wallets.
What is your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 1 hour before the ride. Cancellations within 1 hour may attract a nominal fee.
Are toll charges included?
For outstation trips, toll charges are included in the package price. For local rentals, toll charges may apply separately.
Are your drivers verified?
Yes, all our drivers are background verified, licensed, and trained for professional and safe driving.
